Steps To Take While Practicing Music

1. Warm Up

Make sure you play through any warm ups, for your instrument, before playing through the piece you're learning or will be preforming.

2. Write On Your Music

Write anything and everything, on your sheet music, that will help ensure you play correctly and consistently.

3. Stay Motivated

It can be difficult to stay motivated, or not get bored while practicing. Try breaking up the song into little groups of 1-4 measures, and make it like a game. See if you can play the group 5 times in a row without making a mistake. Remember to play the section slow first, to ensure less mistakes. You can always speed it up later.

4. Develop Muscle Memory

The next thing you know, after playing through the song group by group, you will have gone through the whole song. You will also develop the proper muscle memory to play through the song, even without the sheet music.

5. You're Ready To Play

When you repeat these steps over and over, you will be able to play the song with ease. So much so, that you can show it off to everyone!

Enjoy playing, and keep practicing!!
